Family Lineage Preservation

Welcome to The Q-Continuum Project!

Thank you for being part of something extraordinary. Your contribution today helps preserve our family's story for generations to come.

Our Mission

The Q-Continuum Project is a living family archive designed to preserve not just names and dates, but the context, relationships, and memories that make our lineage meaningful. This is about preventing knowledge decay and ensuring that what we know today doesn't disappear tomorrow.

What to Expect

  • This survey takes time. Set aside 20-45 minutes depending on how much you know.
  • Fill out what you know. It's perfectly okay to leave things blank if you're unsure.
  • Your answers matter. Even partial information helps us build connections and preserve our heritage.
  • This is gentle work. We understand family histories can be complex, sensitive, and incomplete. We've designed this with care.

Survey Overview

You'll be asked to share information about:

  • Yourself (or someone you're adding to our records)
  • Parents, grandparents, and extended family
  • Siblings, children, and spouses
  • Life details: education, career, hobbies, values
  • Photos or documents you'd like to contribute
